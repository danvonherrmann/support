üì∫ TV says "No Signal" or shows a black screen

3. Make sure all cables are firmly connected
4. Try unplugging the TV and satellite receiver for 30 seconds, then plug back in
üéÆ Remote control isn't working
1. Replace the batteries (most common fix)
2. Make sure you're pointing at the satellite receiver, not the TV
3. Press the SAT button at the top to control the receiver
4. Press the TV button at the top to control TV volume
üîá No sound coming from TV
1. Check that TV volume isn't muted or turned down

3. Make sure HDMI cable is fully inserted
4. Try a different HDMI input on the TV
